Description:
The agricultural sector is a cornerstone of India's economy, serving as a vital source of employment and livelihood for a substantial portion of the populace. As the population continues to expand, it becomes imperative to assess the present condition of agriculture and formulate strategic plans to promote sustainable farming methods, secure food supplies, and create ample opportunities for livelihoods among the people.

Expected Solutions:

The aim is to gain a thorough grasp of the existing agricultural landscape, pinpoint obstacles and prospects, and devise strategic measures to advance sustainable farming methods, boost productivity, and elevate the quality of life for the expanding populace. sss
